Jelly Bean Collector!

You will be given a random goal number at the start of the game. When you click on a jellybean, it will add a specific amount of points to your score. Your goal is to click on jellybeans accordingly to arrive at the exact goal total. If you go over, you lose! The value of each jelly bean will reset at the start of each new game. Good Luck!
